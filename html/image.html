rohit sharma

Rohit Sharma is a prominent Indian international cricketer, an elegant right-handed top-order batter, and the former captain of the national team who recently led India to victories in the 2024 T20 World Cup and the 2025 ICC Champions Trophy. He currently features exclusively in ODI cricket after retiring from T20 and Test formats. Known as the 'Hitman' for his big-hitting prowess, he holds several world records, including three double centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs).
